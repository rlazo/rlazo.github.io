Learning some R

Tagged as programming, R

Written on 2014-11-16 21:40:10

This weekend I've been helping my brother with an issue with a R script he needs for his masters. And my God, that was one ugly script. It was pretty obvious that the original author, not my brother, didn't know very well how to write good scripts, neither was interested in learning. A big hairy mess of R code. It was my first time working with R and, while I didn't find it as ugly as I originally though, I hope that I don't have to mess with it too often.
